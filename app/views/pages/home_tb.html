How TreadHunter Works

You can compare hundreds of new & used tire stores, private sellers and their prices, all at once!

  • Search for new and used tires by vehicle or tire size.
  • Filter result by the type of seller, brand, and tire type.
  • Sort results by distance, price, type, quantity, and treadlife.
  • Click a tire listing to see more information.
  • Reserve the tire(s) sending an instant alert to the seller.
